Fitness encompasses a broad range of activities and practices aimed at improving physical health and well-being. It involves engaging in regular exercise, such as cardiovascular activities, strength training, flexibility exercises, and other forms of physical activity. Fitness also includes adopting a balanced and nutritious diet, managing stress, getting sufficient rest and sleep, and maintaining a healthy body weight. It is a holistic approach to overall wellness that promotes strength, endurance, flexibility, cardiovascular health, and mental well-being. Regular fitness activities can contribute to improved physical function, increased energy levels, enhanced mood, reduced risk of chronic diseases, and an overall higher quality of life.
